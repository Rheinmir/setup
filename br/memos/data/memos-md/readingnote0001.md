## 📖 Reading: Deep Work #books #reading

Started Cal Newport's *Deep Work* this week. This passage stopped me:

> "Human beings, it seems, are at their best when immersed deeply in something challenging."

That's the whole pitch in one sentence. The rest of the book is just evidence for it.

Trying an experiment for the next two weeks: no Slack or email until 11am. See what shifts when the morning is mine.
