## 🧩 Try Memos Scratchpad

A lightweight visual canvas for quick thoughts, links, images, files, and small cards before they become polished notes.

[Open Scratchpad →](https://usememos.com/scratchpad)

| Need | Scratchpad helps you |
|------|----------------------|
| Capture fast | Double-click to add notes |
| Arrange freely | Drag cards around |
| Add context | Attach files, links, and images |
| Move around | Zoom and pan without setup |

### Local-first

Your Scratchpad cards stay in your browser. Memos does not access, sync, or store them.
